## 4. English Draft

### App name
- `On the Timeline`

### Short description
- Quickly log movies, series, and books without sign-up, then revisit them in your personal timeline.

### Promotional copy
- Save what you watch and read in seconds.
- On the Timeline helps you build a personal timeline of movies, series, and books without a tedious sign-up flow.
- Keep simple notes, dates, and status updates, then continue on another device with pairing.

### Key features
- Fast logging for movies, series, and books
- Personal timeline of your saved records
- Save notes, dates, and status together
- Connect multiple devices with pairing codes
- Send questions, bug reports, and ideas through the feedback inbox
- Install and use it like an app from your browser

### Store description
- On the Timeline is built for fast and lightweight logging of movies, series, and books.
- Start without creating an account, save what you are watching or reading, and keep short notes alongside each record.
- Your records are collected into a personal timeline so you can easily look back on what you watched or read over time.
- You can also connect multiple devices with pairing codes and keep using the same timeline across devices.

### Suggested keywords

- watch tracker
- reading tracker
- timeline
- personal log
